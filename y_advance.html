Welcome to Advance level Yoga!!


Padahastasana

Padahastasana means keeping the palms down towards the feet. It means hand-to-foot pose. Yogic postures like padahastasana may effectively control headaches, improve concentration and manage stress.

This asan is recommended to be performed 2-3 times every day.

Pavanamuktasana

Pavanamuktasana - The Wind-Releasing Pose. It relieves constipation & flatulence. Invigorates the reproductive system and improves its health.

Practice this for 2-3 rounds a day for better results

Setu Bhandasana

Setu Bandhaasana is a gentle yoga backbend that can be energizing, rejuvenating, or luxuriously restorative and can improve blood flow

You can stay in the pose from 30 seconds to 1 minute for every day.

Shashankasana

Shashankasana may be beneficial to improve heart health, reduce back pain, lowering cholesterol levels and enhance digestion.

You may practice it for 2-3 times a day

Ustrasana

Ustrasana is a chest-opening backbend that is energizing and beneficial. It is a wonderful addition to your sequence since it prevents slouching and eases lower back stiffness.

You can perform it 2 to 4 times wherein they hold this position for 30 to 60 seconds each time.

Vakrasana

Vakrasana, or the spinal twist pose, is a yoga pose with several benefits, such as stretching the stomach, treating diabetes and many more.

One can do it at least 4-5 times a day for at least 2-3 minutes..
