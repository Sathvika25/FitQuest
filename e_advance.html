Welcome to Advance level Exercise!!


Single Arm Row

Single-arm row involves bending forward, pulling weight towards torso with one arm, targeting back muscles for strength and definition.

3 sets of 10 reps per hand at one time.

Russian Twists with Dumbells

Russian twist with kettlebell involves sitting on the floor, rotating torso while holding kettlebell, engaging core muscles for strength and stability.

3 sets of 10 reps per hand at one time.

Romanian Deadlift

Romanian deadlift involves hinging at the hips with slight knee bend, lowering weight, and returning to standing position, targeting hamstrings.

3 sets of 1-2 reps.

Hip Thrust with Dumbells

Hip thrust with dumbbell involves lying on back with knees bent, lifting hips upward, targeting glutes and hamstrings for strength.

3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Kettlebell Lateral Lunges

Kettlebell lateral lunge involves stepping to the side while holding a kettlebell, bending the knee, targeting legs and glutes.

3 sets of 10 reps

Bulgarian Split Squat

Bulgarian split squat involves standing with one foot elevated on a bench, lowering into a lunge to target leg muscles.

10 reps of 3 sets
