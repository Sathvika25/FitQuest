Welcome to Beginner level Exercise!!


Pushups

Pushups are a fundamental bodyweight exercise where one lowers their body to the ground by bending their arms, then pushes back up to the starting position.

3 sets of 10 - 12 reps.

Bench Press

Bench press is a weightlifting exercise where one lies on a bench, lowers a barbell to the chest, then presses upward. It primarily targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps, promoting upper body strength and muscle growth.

.3 sets of 10 reps per hand at same time.

Floor Press

Floor press is a weightlifting exercise done lying on the floor, pressing weights upward, targeting chest, shoulders, and triceps.

3 sets of 10 reps per hand at same time.

Seated Bicep Curl

Seated bicep curls involve lifting weights while seated, targeting and strengthening the biceps muscles for increased arm strength and definition.

3 sets of 7-10 reps per hand at same time..

Body Weight Squat

Bodyweight squats are performed by lowering your body into a squatting position, keeping feet shoulder-width apart, knees aligned over toes, then returning to standing position, engaging leg muscles for strength and stability.

3 sets of 15 reps

Plank

Plank involves holding a push-up position, with forearms on the ground, body straight, engaging core muscles to strengthen abdominal and back.

You may practice this pose for 30 seconds, and if comfortable, you may increase the holding time to 1 minute.
